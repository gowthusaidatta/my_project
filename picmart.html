About Picmart

With over 15 years of experience in the photo frame industry, we take pride in creating personalized products that help you cherish your special moments.

Our journey began in Kathipudi with a small shop and a big dream - to help people preserve their memories in beautiful, customized ways. Today, we serve customers across the region with our wide range of photo frames and personalized gift items.

We believe that every memory is unique, and so should be the way you preserve it. That's why we offer 500+ designs and complete customization options for all our products.
